NEW YORK CITY

"The City of Opportunities"

Information:

  • Located: New York, United States of America
  • Population: ~8,175,133
  • Land area: 303 sq. mi.
  • Average daily temp.: Jan., 31.5° F; July, 76.8° F
  • Almost half of the city's residents over the age of 5 speak a language besides English
  • NYC is the most densely populated major city in the US
  • The city is divided into five boroughs: Manhattan, the Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn and Staten Island

History:

  • NYC was orignally named New Amsterdam by the first settlers to arrive from Europe
  • First settlers arrived in 1624
  • The first colonists were Dutch, fleeing the Spanish Inquisition
  • Peter Minuit purchased the land from the Native Americans in 1625
  • At the start of the 20th century the city's population was over 3 million
  • NYC was the nation's capital from 1789 to 1790
